# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis (Python)

## 📊 Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Diwali sales data using Python. The goal is to understand customer behavior, identify sales trends, and extract meaningful business insights to support decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze customer purchasing patterns during the Diwali season and identify key factors influencing sales such as demographics, location, occupation, and product categories.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python 🐍  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains information about customer demographics, orders, and sales amount.

👉 Includes:
- Customer details (Age, Gender, Marital Status)
- Location (State, Zone)
- Occupation
- Product Category
- Orders and Sales Amount

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
- Removed unnecessary columns (`Status`, `unnamed1`)
- Handled missing values by dropping null entries
- Converted `Amount` column to integer type
- Ensured data consistency for analysis

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Gender-wise analysis of customers and spending
- Age group distribution of buyers
- State-wise sales and order analysis
- Marital status impact on purchasing behavior
- Occupation-based analysis
- Product category performance
- Top-selling products identification

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Female customers contribute more to overall sales
- Age group **26–35** has the highest purchasing activity
- Top states: **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka**
- Married women show higher purchasing power
- Customers from **IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors** spend more
- Top categories: **Food, Clothing, Electronics**

---

## 📌 Conclusion
Married women aged 26–35 from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors, are the most valuable customer segment. They show a strong preference for Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories, making them an ideal target group for marketing and sales strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Insights
- Target marketing campaigns toward high-spending demographics
- Focus on top-performing states for regional promotions
- Promote high-demand product categories
- Create personalized offers for key customer segments
